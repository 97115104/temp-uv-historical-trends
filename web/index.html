    Data: temperature is the ERA5 monthly mean from Open-Meteo; UV is the monthly peak (WHO scale) from NASA POWER (2001–2020, adjusted to the WHO scale) and Open-Meteo (2021+).

    Values reflect the latest complete month (roughly a 1-week to 1-month lag), not today's live conditions. UV from these models reads a little lower than some phone weather apps, and pre-2021 UV is scale-adjusted — so long-run UV trends are indicative. Temperature change is shown in degrees, the standard way to measure warming.
